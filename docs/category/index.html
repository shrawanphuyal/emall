# Categories and Sub categories

METHODGET
URLhttp://emall.com.np/api/categories?levelOne=1&levelTwo=1
AUTHfalse
HEADERS
  • Accept: application/json

Info

  • To get first level categories pass levelOne=0 and levelTwo=0
  • To get second level categories pass levelOne={someInteger} and levelTwo=0
  • To get third level categories pass levelOne={someInteger} and levelTwo={someInteger}

# Products of a category

METHODGET
URLhttp://emall.com.np/api/category-products?levelOne=1&levelTwo=1&levelThree=1
AUTHfalse
HEADERS
  • Accept: application/json

Info

  • To get products of first level categories pass levelOne={someInteger}, levelTwo=0 and levelThree=0
  • To get products of second level categories pass levelOne={someInteger}, levelTwo={someInteger} and levelThree=0
  • To get products of third level categories pass levelOne={someInteger}, levelTwo={someInteger} and levelThree={someInteger}
